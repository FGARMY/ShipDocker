## 2. FAQ Reply Templates (Quick Replies)

Save these in your WhatsApp Business app under **Quick Replies** so your support team can insert them with a single `/` slash command.

### Delivery Time (Metro vs Tier-2)
`Shortcut: /delivery`
> "Hi there! 📦 For Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, etc.), we deliver within **3–4 business days**. For Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, it typically takes **5–7 business days**. Once your order ships, we'll auto-send a tracking link so you can monitor it live. Let us know your Pincode and we'll give you an exact estimate!"

### Return Process
`Shortcut: /returns`
> "Hi! We offer a smooth 7-Day Easy Return policy. 🔄 If your item is damaged or defective, just send us a 10-second video of the issue right here on WhatsApp! We will approve the return instantly and schedule a free doorstep pickup within 48 hours. Refunds are processed immediately after pickup."

### Product Authenticity
`Shortcut: /genuine`
> "Hello! ✅ Yes, absolutely. All ShipDocker products are 100% genuine and sourced directly from verified manufacturers. We do not sell fakes or replicas. This is why we confidently offer our 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee and 256-bit secure checkout. You're in safe hands!"

### Cash on Delivery (COD)
`Shortcut: /cod`
> "Hi! 💵 Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across 19,000+ pincodes in India! There is a nominal ₹49 handling fee for COD orders to prevent fake bookings, but you can also choose UPI or Cards at checkout to avoid this small fee completely. Ready to place your order?"

### GST Invoices
`Shortcut: /gst`
> "Hello! 🧾 Yes, we provide B2B compliant GST invoices on all our orders so you can claim your input tax credit. Once your order is successfully placed, please reply here with your Order ID and your Company GSTIN, and our automated system will email the official invoice to you within 24 hours."
